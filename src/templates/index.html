The idea

Tags tell you what a fic is tagged.
They don't tell you what it reads like.

Two fics can share every tag on AO3 and still turn out to have little in common — the tag only tells you the ship, not what the fic does with it. Opsis looks past the tag list itself: it compares summaries directly, checks fandom and relationship overlap, and factors in how well a fic has already landed with readers. Then it hands you the ones that actually sit closest to what you just read.
